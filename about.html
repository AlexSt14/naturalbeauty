About

Makeup has been my playground since childhood, a canvas for joyful expression. From blushing brides to bold everyday looks, I translate dreams into reality. My passion fueled formal training, including at Hollie Fitzmartin Academy, and spills over into henna artistry, where I sculpt brows and empower individuals. Countless experiences honed my skills, allowing me to personalize each touch, whether creating timeless bridal glows or collaborating on unique everyday looks. Witnessing confidence blossom with each stroke fuels my passion.

This isn't just a profession; it's a dedication to using my expertise to make the ordinary extraordinary, one transformation at a time. Let's connect and explore your beauty journey!
